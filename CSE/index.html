About Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), established in 1994, has earned NBA accreditation five times and boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure. It has received funding from prominent agencies like AICTE, UGC, DRDO, ISRO, and BRNS, and maintains strategic partnerships with leading organizations such as L&T Technologies, GE Healthcare, Zeuron.ai, and Roots Industries, focusing on AI & ML, AR & VR, software development, and more. With Centres of Excellence in AR/VR, GE Healthcare, Virtusa, and a GPU Learning Centre, the department is driven by a highly qualified faculty committed to advancing research and innovation in computer science.

About Elements

Elements , organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Association of Computer Engineers at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, is a renowned technical symposium promoting innovation since 2007. It offers a platform for students to showcase their skills through technical and creative events, fostering collaboration and new ideas. Elements continues to inspire future innovators and leaders in computer science.

General Instruction

  • Every participant must have registered individually before reporting to the event. If a team has 2 or more members, each member must register individually.
  • All participants are required to follow the appropriate dress code (Boys - Shirt/Pant, Girls - Kurthi/Chudidhar). T-Shirts are not allowed.
  • All registered participants must collect their respective Elements 2k24 registration kit from the registration desk on the day of the event.
  • Please ensure you carry your college ID card, as ID verification will be conducted before the event begins. Participants are required to keep their ID card with them for the duration of the event.
  • Late entries for events will not be permitted. Participants are also advised to report to the campus 30 minutes prior to the event for a hassle-free experience.
  • Important Guidelines and Information regarding the Symposium will be conveyed at the Inauguration Event (October 4th) of Elements 2k24, so participants must attend the same without fail.
  • Any discrepancy during the event should be immediately reported to the event convenor, and necessary actions will be taken. Any late response will not be taken into consideration.
  • The judge's decision is final for all the events and will not be subject to further discussion. Indulging in any malpractices will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • All participants should pay the general registration fee to attend the events.
  • Any form of inappropriate behavior will be highly condemned, and necessary actions will be taken.
  • Food and refreshments will be provided to all participants during the event.
  • All participants will receive a participation certificate upon completion of the event.
  • All events will conclude by October 4th, and the prize winners will be announced immediately at the valedictory event (October 4th) of Elements 2k24.
